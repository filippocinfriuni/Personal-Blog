What I suggest

I suggest to follow these steps:
  • Understand the rules
  • Play for fun some games
  • Understand the fundamentals
    • Understand the basic principles of the endgames
    • Understand the basic tactics
    • Understand the basic principles of openings
  • Have a background of chess players
    • See some chess events
    • See some past chess matches
  • Choose some inspiring players to learn more advanced technique from
