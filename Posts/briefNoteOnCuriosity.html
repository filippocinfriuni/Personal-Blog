A Brief Note on Curiosity

#Curiosity

Curiosity is my driving factor to discover new things and meet new people. My curiosity is fuelled by the fact that time is never wasted. I either come upon something I love or learn what I don’t like. These pieces of information are helpful when deciding what to do with my time and energy.
Being curious has effectively enabled me to meet new friends and interesting people.
The hurdle is being truly curious without bias. Often I let my prejudice dictate where to go or let others’ opinions leave an impression on a particular thing.
